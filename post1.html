Personal SWOT Analysis

Strengths

I'm really good at solving problems, especially in programming. I can also communicate well with others, which makes teamwork easier. Additionally, I'm a logical thinker and love to learn, which helps me understand things systematically.

Weaknesses

often leading me to seek perfection in my work. Sometimes, I overthink things, wanting everything to be perfect I can be quite critical of my own performance, which sometimes results in spending more time on tasks than necessary. Another challenge I also tend to have lots of ideas but struggle to stick to just one.

Opportunities

I see opportunities in my problem-solving skills. They can help me handle complex tasks and find creative solutions. I'm working on balancing my overthinking to do thorough work without delays. My many ideas could lead to creative solutions. However, I need to avoid letting perfectionism cause procrastination or missed deadlines.

Threat

The risk with overthinking is missing out on time-sensitive opportunities due to delays. Struggling to stick to one idea might lead to a lack of focus and reduced productivity. If perfectionism isn't managed, it could cause procrastination and missed deadlines, which is a threat to my academic or career progress. I'm actively working on these to avoid these threats
